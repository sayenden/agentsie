Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my financial data?

We use bank-grade security with read-only access through Plaid. We never store passwords or account numbers, and all data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

When will the full service be available?

We're currently in private beta with select users. Join our early access list to be notified when we open to more users. We expect broader availability in Q1 2026.

How much does the service cost?

Our pricing will be announced closer to public launch. Early access users will receive special pricing. The service is designed to pay for itself through increased rewards earnings.

Which credit cards do you support?

We support all major credit cards from Chase, American Express, Citi, Capital One, Bank of America, and hundreds of other issuers through our Plaid integration.
